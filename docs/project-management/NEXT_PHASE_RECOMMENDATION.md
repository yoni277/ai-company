## Why Phase 5C is deferred

Phase 5C **Financial Health Engine** would score and classify financial health across the portfolio. With **75% mock revenue** and **75% mock funnel** data, health scores would:

- Imply precision that does not exist  
- Rank mock projects alongside FoodTruck-IL  
- Create false confidence for capital allocation  

**Phase 5B** already delivers **visibility** (revenue + financial snapshots + live/mock labels). That is the correct ceiling until data maturity improves.

**Phase 5C.1 (CEO Operating System)** is complete and addresses **execution loop** without health scoring — directives, decisions, owner, due date, tracker.
